# Factory Strider's minimum and maximum difficulties

To submit a run to the **No Major Glitches** subcategory (100%, Any% or Extract%), you **must** play a patch after **1.004.000** and comply with the **current NMG rules**. You can submit a run from any patch to the **Unrestricted** subcategory (100%, Any% or Extract%) and use any glitch/exploit/skip.

- After **1.004.000**: &nbsp; ![Hard v2](/static/blob/1n7v4p9n.png) &nbsp; 5 | HARD &nbsp; · &nbsp; ![Extreme](/static/blob/1n84k2ve.png) &nbsp; 6 | EXTREME.
