## About the Megacity skip

Runs taking place in **Terminids Megacities** with a **broken generation** of **seconday objectives** must only be submitted to the **Unrestricted** subcategory. They are **valid** as long as the number of **optional objectives** shown as **cleared** in the stats screen matches the number of **valid optional objectives icons**.
