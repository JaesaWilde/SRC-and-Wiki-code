# How to play Meridia and Gloom missions

In both cases, you will need the [DarkFluid Launcher](https://github.com/WernherVonKerman/DarkfluidLauncher/releases). in **Assets**, download **darkfluidlauncher.exe** and execute it. It may appear as a threat if you don't build it yourself because it's bundled with **Frida**.

## 1. Meridia missions

- ![Activate Terminid Control System](/static/blob/1n7v4o5n.png) &nbsp; **Activate Terminid Control System**
- ![Deactiave Terminid Control System](/static/blob/1n7v4o5n.png) &nbsp; **Deactivate Terminid Control System**
- ![Deploy Dark Fluid](/static/blob/xzd2wj5e.png) &nbsp; **Deploy Dark Fluid**

To play them, you will need to **downpatch** your game. Follow this [step by step guide](https://www.speedrun.com/Helldivers2/guides/zf9n8) to know how to proceed (useful to access patched **glitches**, **exploits** and **skips** too).
__________

- They work from **1.000.300** to **1.001.104**.
- From **1.000.300** to **1.000.405**, the Maximum Difficulty is &nbsp; ![Helldive v2](/static/blob/rnqv64we.png) &nbsp; **9 | HELLDIVE**.
- From **1.001.002** to **1.001.104**, the Maximum Difficulty is &nbsp; ![Super Helldive v2](/static/blob/kzwmp48n.png) &nbsp; **10 | SUPER HELLDIVE**.

You can play on **any** of these patches to run the following categories:

- **Any%**
- **100% Unrestricted**

To submit a run to the **100% No Major Glitches** category, you **must** play on patch **1.000.305**, use **none of the glitches** found in this version and show **all POVs**.
__________

Once you've downloaded a **working patch**, Launch the **DarkFluid Launcher** and:

- Select ***Darkfluid Missions*** to play &nbsp; ![Deploy Dark Fluid](/static/blob/xzd2wj5e.png) &nbsp; **Deploy Dark Fluid**.
- Select ***TCS Missions*** to play &nbsp; ![Activate Terminid Control System](/static/blob/1n7v4o5n.png) &nbsp; **Activate Terminid Control System**.
- Select ***Deactivate TCS Missions*** to play &nbsp; ![Deactiave Terminid Control System](/static/blob/1n7v4o5n.png) &nbsp; **Deactivate Terminid Control System**.
__________
